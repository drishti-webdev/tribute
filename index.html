Biography

Indira Priyadarshini Gandhi was an Indian politician and the first and only female Prime Minister of India. She served as Prime Minister from 1966 to 1977 and again from 1980 until her assassination in 1984. She was the daughter of Jawaharlal Nehru, India's first Prime Minister.

Indira Gandhi was known for her strong leadership, determination, and courage. She played a major role in strengthening India politically and economically. Her leadership during challenging times made her one of the most powerful leaders in Indian history.

She continues to inspire millions of people, especially women, to lead with confidence, strength, and dedication toward their nation.
