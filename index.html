Biography

Indira Gandhi was the first and only female Prime Minister of India. She served from 1966 to 1977 and again from 1980 until 1984. She was known for her strong political decisions, leadership qualities, and dedication to the nation.


What I admire most about Indira Gandhi is her courage and confidence. She led India during challenging times and made bold decisions to protect and strengthen the country. Her leadership continues to inspire generations of women and leaders across the world.


She remains one of the most powerful and influential leaders in Indian history. Her legacy reminds us that determination, intelligence, and bravery can change the future.
