Biography

Indira Gandhi was the first and only female Prime Minister of India. She was known for her strong leadership, determination, and courage. She served as Prime Minister from 1966 to 1977 and again from 1980 until her assassination in 1984.

What I admire most about Indira Gandhi is her fearless personality and her dedication to serving the nation. She made bold decisions and stood strong during difficult times, showing great leadership and confidence.

She inspired millions of women across the world by proving that women can lead nations with strength, intelligence, and determination. Her legacy continues to inspire future generations.
